# vows.you

A privacy-first wedding vow helper that turns a few thoughtful prompts into ceremony-ready vows that actually sound like you.

## What it does

- Helps you get started with guided prompts (no blank page panic)
- Shapes your answers into a clear vow structure (opening → story → promises → close)
- Keeps your draft focused and readable (roughly 1–2 minutes by default)
- Lets you refine and practice until it feels right

If you want the full experience, visit <a href="https://www.vows.you/" target="_blank">vows.you</a>.

## Why it exists

Writing wedding vows is personal, but the process can be stressful:
- People know what they feel, but don’t know how to structure it
- Generic templates can feel fake
- Time pressure makes it worse

vows.you is designed to give you structure and guidance while keeping your voice in control.

## Core principles

- **Clarity over cleverness**: simple words that feel true
- **Your voice**: we guide, you write
- **Calm confidence**: reduce stress with structure and practice
- **Inclusive by design**: prompts work for all couples
- **Privacy-first**: your writing stays yours
