## Getting Started with vows.you

### Start Writing — No Sign-Up Required
You can jump straight into the workspace and start writing without creating an account or entering your email. Just open the app and go.

If you want to save your progress, you can create a free account at any time.

### Creating Your First Draft
1. Go to the **Workspace**.
2. Choose a **tone** — heartfelt, funny, poetic, playful, traditional, or modern.
3. Pick an **outline** to give your vows structure (or go freeform).
4. Follow the **guided prompts** to pull out your stories, memories, and promises.
5. Your vows will take shape as you write — no blank page required.

### Choosing a Tone
Not sure what tone fits? Here's a quick guide:
- **Heartfelt:** Sincere, emotional, classic wedding vow energy.
- **Light & Funny:** Opens with humor, lands on sincerity.
- **Poetic:** Lyrical and expressive — for the writers and romantics.
- **Modern Minimal:** Distilled and direct — every word earns its place.

You can change your tone at any time without losing your progress.

### Saving Your Work
- **Free accounts** can save 1 draft.
- **Full Access** unlocks unlimited drafts so you can explore different versions.

Your work is saved automatically as you type.
