## Billing & Guarantee

### Pricing
vows.you has two simple plans:

- **Free Forever ($0):** Guided prompts, basic structure guide, 1 saved draft, read time estimate, and text export.
- **Full Access ($129 one-time):** Everything in Free, plus unlimited drafts, practice mode, tone suggestions, personal writing coach, print-ready export, priority support, and a ceremony day checklist.

Full Access is a **one-time payment**. No subscriptions. No recurring charges. You pay once and keep access forever.

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
If vows.you doesn't help you write vows you're proud of, we'll refund your purchase within 30 days. No questions asked.

To request a refund:
1. Email us at **support@vows.you** within 30 days of your purchase.
2. We'll process your refund — no hoops, no forms, no hassle.

We believe in this product. If it doesn't work for you, we don't want your money.

### Payment Methods
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely.

### Receipts
A receipt is sent to your email automatically after purchase. If you need a copy, reach out to **support@vows.you**.
