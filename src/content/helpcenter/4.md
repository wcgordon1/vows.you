## Support & Troubleshooting

### Contacting Us
The fastest way to reach us is by email:
- **Email:** hello@vows.you
- We respond to most requests within **24 hours**.
- **Priority support** is available for Full Access users.

### Common Issues

#### My draft didn't save
Drafts save automatically as you type. If something didn't save:
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Refresh the page  -  your latest auto-save should still be there.
3. If you're on the Free plan, remember that only 1 draft is saved at a time.

#### Export isn't working
- Make sure your vows have content before exporting.
- Try a different browser if the download doesn't start.
- If the issue persists, email us with your browser name and version.

#### Page is loading slowly
- Clear your browser cache and cookies.
- Try a different browser or device.
- Check if your internet connection is stable.

### Mobile Access
vows.you works in any modern mobile browser  -  no app download required. Your workspace, drafts, and all features are fully accessible on your phone or tablet.

### Feature Requests & Feedback
We'd love to hear from you. If you have ideas for how we can make vows.you better, drop us a line at **hello@vows.you**.
