Your vows deserve to look as good on paper as they sound out loud. With **print-ready export**, you can generate a beautifully formatted version of your vows — ready to fold into your pocket, place on the podium, or tuck into a keepsake.

**Key Highlights:**

- **Clean Formatting:** Your vows exported with elegant typography, proper spacing, and a layout designed for easy reading at the ceremony.
- **Multiple Formats:** Export as PDF or plain text — whatever works best for your ceremony setup.
- **Ceremony Day Checklist:** A simple checklist to make sure you've got everything covered — from final edits to backup copies to timing your read-through one last time.
- **Keepsake Ready:** The formatted export doubles as a beautiful memento you can frame or save alongside your wedding photos.

This update is all about making sure you feel prepared, polished, and ready to deliver vows you're proud of on the day that matters most.
